About Me

Welcome! Thank you for stopping by.

I am a few weeks away from graduating from Weber State University and I am excited to begin a career in technical writing. Technical writing generates a relationship between a technical product and its audience through documentation. I truly enjoy taking that complex information and reducing it into easily digestible pieces for broad audiences. While I would not claim to know everything about everything, I am an inquisitive individual with a desire to know more; technical writing supports that desire.
